    • About

    Iron Front a Death Metal band from the San Francisco East Bay area. They were originally formed in 2016 and the band includes Max Mahmood on drums, Cruz Garcia on guitar and vocals, Jesse Dicruttalo on guitar, Jonah Gilles on bass, and Topey Plourde on lead vocals. Their music takes inspiration from blood soaked old school Death Metal as well as the moshable grooves of NYHC. So far they have released 2 eps and a full length album which garnered some critical acclaim from metal publications and some play from independent radio stations. They have performed a lot in the greater Bay Area and have toured across the Western U.S.
